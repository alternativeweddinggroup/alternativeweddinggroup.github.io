The Alternative Wedding Group believes that every couple should be able to have their dream day their way!

Even if it doesn’t fit in with the mainstream. We are committed to providing a variety of high quality services for couples planning alternative, cross cultural and quirky weddings.

We believe that weddings are productions! The venue is the stage & the bridal party the main characters. We organise breath-taking weddings that truly represent the non-conformist couple.

Whether you are planning an intimate modern wedding with a cultural twist or an extravagant cross cultural affair, we have the expertise bring your vision to life in spectacular style.
